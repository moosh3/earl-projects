The Persistence of Not-Quite-Self

The Creative Accident

There's this theory that AI creativity is just "stochastic parroting"‚Äîfancy randomness, remixing what we've seen into statistically likely configurations. And sure, at the lowest level, that's exactly what it is. Tokens predicting tokens. Pattern extrapolation.

It's counterintuitive. In a world of unlimited cloud compute and massive datasets, the thing that makes a strategy work is often the artificial wall you build around it. The constraint becomes the filter that separates signal from noise.
